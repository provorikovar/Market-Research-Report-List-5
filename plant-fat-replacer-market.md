<p><ul><li>Carbohydrate-based</li><li>Protein-based</li><li>Lipid-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plant fat replacers are categorized into three main types based on their composition: </p><p>1. Carbohydrate-based fat replacers utilize ingredients like starches and fibers to mimic the mouthfeel and texture of fats in food products. </p><p>2. Protein-based fat replacers use proteins such as soy or wheat to replace the fat content in food while still providing a similar creamy texture. </p><p>3. Lipid-based fat replacers contain ingredients like oils or emulsifiers to replicate the taste and functionality of fats in products. Each type offers unique benefits for manufacturers looking to reduce fat content in their products.</p></p>
