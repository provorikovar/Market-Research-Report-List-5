<p><p>Milling-Turning Centers are categorized into two main types based on their orientation: Vertical and Horizontal. Vertical milling-turning centers have the spindle mounted vertically, allowing for easier chip evacuation and better access to the workpiece. Horizontal milling-turning centers have the spindle mounted horizontally, making them more suitable for heavy-duty cutting and longer workpieces. Both types have their unique advantages and are used in different industries based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
