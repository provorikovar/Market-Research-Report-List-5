<p><p>Industrial process chillers are cooling systems used in various industries to regulate temperatures during manufacturing processes. The market is divided into two main types: Air Cooled Chillers and Water Cooled Chillers. Air Cooled Chillers use ambient air to remove heat from the system, making them more cost-effective and easier to maintain. On the other hand, Water Cooled Chillers use water as a cooling medium, providing more efficient cooling but requiring additional equipment for water circulation. Both types cater to different industrial applications based on specific cooling requirements.</p></p>
