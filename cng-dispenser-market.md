<p><p>CNG dispensers come in two main types: Fast Fill and Time Fill. Fast Fill dispensers are designed for quick refueling of natural gas vehicles, making them ideal for high-traffic refueling stations or fleets with tight schedules. Time Fill dispensers are slower but more cost-effective, as they allow vehicles to refuel overnight. This makes Time Fill dispensers popular among fleets with centralized refueling locations where vehicles can be parked for extended periods. Both types serve different needs within the CNG market, providing flexibility for various types of users.</p></p>
