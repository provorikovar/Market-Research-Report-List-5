Curvilinear transducers have a curved shape for broader imaging, phased array transducers have multiple small elements for better resolution, endocavity transducers are specifically designed for use in body cavities, and linear transducers have a straight shape for superficial imaging. Each type offers unique capabilities for different medical imaging applications.</p></p>
