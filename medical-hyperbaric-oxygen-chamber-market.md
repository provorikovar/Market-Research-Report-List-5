<p><p>Medical hyperbaric oxygen chambers are classified into two types based on the gas used for pressurization - Air Pressurized and Oxygen Pressurized. In Air Pressurized chambers, the patient breathes in oxygen while the overall atmospheric pressure is increased. On the other hand, Oxygen Pressurized chambers supply pure oxygen to the patient at higher atmospheric pressures. Both types of chambers are used for various medical conditions such as decompression sickness, non-healing wounds, and carbon monoxide poisoning, to facilitate faster healing and recovery.</p></p>
