<p><p>Poly cotton fabrics are available in different market types based on weight and weave. By weight, they are classified as 150 g/m², 300 g/m², and others. The weight of the fabric determines its thickness and durability, with 150 g/m² being lighter and suitable for summer clothing, while 300 g/m² is heavier and ideal for winter garments. The weave of the fabric affects its appearance and texture, with plain weave offering a simple, balanced look, twill weave providing a diagonal pattern, and satin weave creating a smooth, glossy finish.</p></p>
