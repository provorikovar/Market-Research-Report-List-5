<p><ul><li>In-Line</li><li>Surface Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymer resettable fuses are widely used in electronic devices to prevent damage from overcurrent situations. In-Line resettable fuses are designed to be soldered directly onto a circuit board, providing a compact and efficient solution for protecting individual components. Surface mount resettable fuses are mounted directly onto the surface of a circuit board, eliminating the need for through-hole components and reducing assembly time. Both types offer reliable overcurrent protection and are commonly found in consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
