<p><strong>The Outdoor Step Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Sensor</li><li>Vibration Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Outdoor step sensors use different types of technology to measure and track steps taken. Gravity sensors utilize the Earth's gravitational pull to detect movement and count steps accurately. Vibration sensors, on the other hand, detect vibrations caused by steps and convert them into step counts. Both types of sensors are commonly used in wearable devices such as fitness trackers to monitor physical activity levels. Gravity sensors are more accurate for detecting sudden movements, while vibration sensors are better at capturing subtle movements.</p></p>
