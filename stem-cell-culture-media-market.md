Embryonic stem culture media is specifically designed for the growth and maintenance of embryonic stem cells. Tissue-specific stem culture media is tailored for the culture of stem cells from specific tissues. Mesenchymal stem culture media supports the growth of mesenchymal stem cells derived from sources like bone marrow or adipose tissue. Induced pluripotent stem culture media is formulated for reprogrammed adult cells to exhibit characteristics of embryonic stem cells.</p></p>
