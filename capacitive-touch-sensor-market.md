<p><ul><li>Surface Capacitive Sensing</li><li>Projected Capacitive Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive Touch Sensor market encompasses two major types: Surface Capacitive Sensing and Projected Capacitive Sensing. Surface Capacitive Sensing technology involves a conductive coating placed on a non-conductive material, allowing for touch detection. Projected Capacitive Sensing uses electrodes to create a grid that can detect touch through materials like glass. Both technologies are widely used in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications due to their high sensitivity, durability, and accuracy in touch detection.</p></p>
