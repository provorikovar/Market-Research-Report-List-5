<p><ul><li>Homo-polyoxymethylene</li><li>Co-polyoxymethylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>POM Sheets are commonly available in two main market types: Homo-polyoxymethylene (homopolymer) and Co-polyoxymethylene (copolymer). Homo-polyoxymethylene sheets are made of a single type of polymer, providing high stiffness, strength, and heat resistance. On the other hand, Co-polyoxymethylene sheets are made of a combination of two different polymers, offering improved impact resistance and stress-crack resistance. Both types are widely used in various industries such as automotive, electrical, and consumer goods due to their excellent mechanical properties.</p></p>
