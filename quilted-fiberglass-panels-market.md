<p><ul><li>One-sided</li><li>Double-sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quilted fiberglass panels are commonly available in two types: one-sided and double-sided. One-sided panels have sound-absorbing fiberglass on only one side, making them suitable for walls where sound absorption is only necessary in one direction. On the other hand, double-sided panels have fiberglass on both sides, providing enhanced sound absorption capabilities in both directions. Depending on the specific acoustic requirements of a space, either one-sided or double-sided quilted fiberglass panels may be chosen for installation.</p></p>
