<p><p>Suction excavators, also known as vacuum excavators, come in three main types: liquid suctioning only, liquid and dry suctioning, and high velocity. Liquid suctioning only models are designed for removing liquids such as water or sludge. Liquid and dry suctioning models can handle both liquids and solid materials like dirt or debris. High velocity suction excavators are specifically built for fast and efficient excavation, making them ideal for large-scale projects where speed is crucial. Each type caters to different applications and industries based on their unique capabilities.</p></p>
