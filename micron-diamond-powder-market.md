<p><p>Micron diamond powder is categorized into two main types: polycrystalline diamond powder and monocrystalline diamond powder. Polycrystalline diamond powder is composed of multiple small diamond crystals, offering high toughness and wear resistance. On the other hand, monocrystalline diamond powder consists of single crystal diamonds, providing superior hardness and precision cutting capabilities. Both types of micron diamond powder cater to various industrial applications such as cutting, grinding, polishing, and drilling due to their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
