<p><p>Expansion bolts are a type of fastener used for securely attaching items to concrete, brick, or other solid materials. Stud anchors are threaded rods with an expandable sleeve that wedges into the base material when tightened. Sleeve anchors have a cylindrical sleeve that expands when the bolt is tightened. Wedge anchors feature a cone-shaped end that expands to create a secure hold. Other types of expansion bolts include strike anchors, drop-in anchors, and machine screw anchors, each designed for specific applications.</p></p>
