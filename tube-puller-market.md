<p><p>Tube pullers are tools used to remove tubes from heat exchangers and other tubing systems. There are two main types of tube puller markets: manual pullers and hydraulic pullers. </p><p>Manual pullers are typically more affordable and easier to use for smaller tube removal projects. They require physical effort to operate but are suitable for simpler tasks. On the other hand, hydraulic pullers are more powerful and efficient, making them ideal for heavy-duty tube extraction jobs. They are operated with hydraulic pressure, which provides greater force and control for removing stubborn tubes.</p></p>
