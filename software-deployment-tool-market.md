<p><p>Software Deployment Tool market is divided into two main types - On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise deployment tools are installed and run on the company's own servers and infrastructure, providing more control over customization and data security, but requiring more maintenance and resources. Cloud-Based deployment tools are hosted and managed by third-party providers, offering scalability, accessibility, and cost-effectiveness, without the need for extensive infrastructure.
