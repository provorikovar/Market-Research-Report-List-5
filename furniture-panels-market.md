<p><p>Furniture Panels market is comprised of various types, including Particleboard (PB), Medium density fibreboard (MDF), High density fibreboard (HDF), Oriented strand board (OSB), and Plywood. Particleboard is made from wood particles bonded together, MDF is made from wood fibers compressed under high pressure, HDF is a denser version of MDF, OSB is made from shredded wood strands arranged in layers, and Plywood is made from thin layers of wood veneer stacked together. Each type has specific characteristics and uses in the furniture industry.</p></p>
