<p><p>Sterilization retorts are used in food processing to eliminate harmful bacteria in packaged foods. There are two main types of sterilization retorts: static retorts and rotary retorts. Static retorts are stationary vessels where products are heated evenly. Rotary retorts, on the other hand, use a rotating mechanism to ensure an even distribution of heat. Both types ensure that foods are safely sterilized before packaging, extending their shelf life and maintaining quality. The choice between static and rotary retorts depends on the specific needs of the food processing operation.</p></p>
