<p><p>Embolization Devices market includes various types such as Embolic Coils, Liquid Agents, Embolic Protection Devices (EPDs), Flow Diverters, and Access Devices, all used in minimally invasive procedures to treat conditions like aneurysms and tumors. Embolic Coils are coils inserted to block blood flow to a specific area, Liquid Agents are injected to block blood vessels, EPDs are used to capture embolic debris, Flow Diverters divert blood flow away from an aneurysm, and Access Devices provide access for the delivery of other embolization devices.</p></p>
