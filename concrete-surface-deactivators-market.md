<p><p>Concrete Surface Deactivators Market is categorized into two types - Organic Agents and Inorganic Agents. Organic Agents are made from natural oils or fatty acids and work by preventing the setting of concrete at the surface. Inorganic Agents, on the other hand, are derived from mineral acids or salts and are used to chemically react with the cement paste, creating a roughened surface. Both types of agents are used in construction projects to achieve different textures and finishes on concrete surfaces.</p></p>
