<p><p>Vacuum emulsifying machines are categorized into four main types: Oil Phase Mixer, Water Phase Mixer, Vacuum System, and Hydraulic Lifting System. The Oil Phase Mixer is designed to mix oil-based ingredients effectively, while the Water Phase Mixer is ideal for blending water-based components. The Vacuum System ensures air bubbles are removed during the emulsification process, while the Hydraulic Lifting System allows for easy adjustment of the machine's height. Each type plays a crucial role in achieving high-quality emulsions for various industries.</p></p>
