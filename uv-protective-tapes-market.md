<p><p>UV protective tapes are classified into two types based on their adhesive properties: single side and double sides. Single side UV protective tapes have adhesive coating on only one side, making them ideal for applications where only one side of the surface needs protection. On the other hand, double sides UV protective tapes have adhesive coating on both sides, providing enhanced protection for surfaces that need to be completely shielded from UV rays. Both types of tapes offer effective UV protection and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
