<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial smoke detector market offers various types of detectors to suit different needs. Photoelectric smoke detectors use light sensors to detect smoke particles, making them effective at detecting smoldering fires. Dual sensor detectors combine both photoelectric and ionization technology for comprehensive smoke detection. Ionization smoke detectors use radioactive particles to ionize the air, making them sensitive to fast-flaming fires. Each type of detector caters to specific fire risks, providing options for commercial spaces to ensure effective fire safety measures.</p></p>
