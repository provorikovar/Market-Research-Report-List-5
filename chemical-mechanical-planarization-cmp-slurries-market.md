<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP) slurries are categorized into Prestonian Type and Non-Prestonian Type markets based on the removal mechanism of material during the polishing process. Prestonian Type slurries primarily remove material at a constant rate regardless of pressure, while Non-Prestonian Type slurries remove material at a rate that varies with pressure. Each type caters to different polishing requirements and surface finish specifications, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable option for their specific CMP applications.</p></p>
