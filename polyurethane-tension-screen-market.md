<p><p>Polyurethane tension screens are used for filtering and separating materials in various industries such as mining, construction, and agriculture. Transversal tension screens are designed with the wires running perpendicular to the flow of material, enabling efficient particle separation. Longitudinal tension screens have wires running parallel to the flow of material, offering high capacity and efficiency in screening operations. Both types of tension screens are durable, reliable, and provide high performance in demanding applications.</p></p>
