<p><p>Straw blankets are erosion control products that help prevent soil erosion on slopes. Single net straw blankets consist of a single netting layer that holds the straw in place, while double net straw blankets have two netting layers for added durability. Single net straw blankets are more affordable and suitable for less steep slopes, while double net straw blankets are designed for steeper slopes and areas with higher water flow. Both types are effective in promoting vegetation growth and reducing erosion.</p></p>
