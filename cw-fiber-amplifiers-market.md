<p><ul><li>Broadband</li><li>Single Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CW fiber amplifiers are commonly used in two market types: broadband and single frequency. Broadband amplifiers are designed to amplify a wide range of wavelengths, making them versatile for various applications. On the other hand, single frequency amplifiers are optimized to amplify only a specific wavelength, providing high power output in a narrow spectral range. Broadband amplifiers are popular for their flexibility, while single frequency amplifiers are favored for their high spectral purity and efficiency in specific applications.</p></p>
