<p><p>Machine vises are available in three main market types: single station, dual station, and multiple station. Single station vises are designed for holding one workpiece at a time, making them ideal for small-scale projects. Dual station vises have the capability to hold two workpieces simultaneously, increasing efficiency for medium-sized projects. Multiple station vises can hold multiple workpieces at once, allowing for high-volume production. Each market type caters to different needs and requirements, providing options for various applications in the machining industry.</p></p>
