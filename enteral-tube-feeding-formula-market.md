Monomeric formulas provide predigested nutrients for patients with compromised digestive systems. Disease-specific formulas are tailored to the specific nutritional requirements of patients with certain medical conditions such as diabetes or renal failure. These different types of enteral tube feeding formulas ensure that patients receive the necessary nutrients to support their health and recovery.</p></p>
