<p><p>Rubber repair adhesives can be categorized into two main types: hot bond and cold bond. Hot bond adhesives require heat for the adhesive to cure and bond effectively, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. On the other hand, cold bond adhesives do not require heat and can cure at room temperature, making them more convenient and versatile for a wide range of applications. Both types of adhesives offer strong bonding capabilities for repairing rubber materials.</p></p>
