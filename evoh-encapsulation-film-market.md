<p><p>EVOH encapsulation film is widely used in the packaging industry due to its excellent barrier properties. There are two main types of EVOH resins used in the market: vinyl alcohol and ethylene. Vinyl alcohol-based EVOH resins provide high gas barrier properties, making them ideal for food packaging applications. On the other hand, ethylene-based EVOH resins offer good chemical resistance, making them suitable for industrial packaging. Both types of EVOH encapsulation film play a crucial role in extending the shelf life of products and protecting them from external factors.</p></p>
