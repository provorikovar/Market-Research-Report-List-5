<p><p>Digital metering pumps are innovative devices used to accurately dispense precise amounts of fluids in a variety of industries. There are two main types of digital metering pumps - electromagnetic drive pumps and motor drive pumps. Electromagnetic drive pumps utilize a magnetic field to control the movement of the piston, allowing for precise and consistent fluid dispensing. Motor drive pumps, on the other hand, use a motor to drive the piston, providing high pressure and flow rates. Both types offer reliable and efficient fluid metering solutions for various applications.</p></p>
