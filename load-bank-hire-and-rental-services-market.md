<p><p>Load bank hire and rental services market types include resistive load bank, reactive load bank, and resistive/reactive load bank. Resistive load banks simulate real-life electrical loads by converting electrical energy to heat, while reactive load banks simulate inductive or capacitive loads. Resistive/reactive load banks combine both resistive and reactive elements to test complete power systems. These different types cater to various testing requirements and offer flexibility in the load testing process for a wide range of industries.</p></p>
