Single Channel EVOA is designed to attenuate the optical signal in one channel, while 4 Channel EVOA can simultaneously attenuate signals in four channels. Both types offer precision control over signal intensity, making them essential components in modern optical communication networks for maintaining signal integrity and ensuring efficient data transmission.</p></p>
