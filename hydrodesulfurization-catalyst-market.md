<p><ul><li>Load Type</li><li>Non-Load Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrodesulfurization catalysts can be classified into two main types based on their load capacity: Load Type and Non-Load Type. Load Type catalysts have a higher load capacity, allowing them to adsorb more sulfur impurities from the feedstock. On the other hand, Non-Load Type catalysts have a lower load capacity but are more effective in desulfurizing the feedstock due to their higher activity level. Both types play a crucial role in the hydrodesulfurization process, offering different benefits depending on the specific requirements of the refining process.</p></p>
