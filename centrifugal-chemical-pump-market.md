<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Mechanical</li><li>Solenoid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal chemical pumps are classified into three main types based on their functioning mechanisms: hydraulic, mechanical, and solenoid. Hydraulic pumps operate using the principles of fluid dynamics to generate centrifugal force. Mechanical pumps involve the use of mechanical components such as gears and pistons to create the pumping action. Solenoid pumps work by utilizing an electric current to control the movement of a solenoid which in turn operates the pump. Each type of pump has its own advantages and is suited for specific applications in the chemical industry.</p></p>
