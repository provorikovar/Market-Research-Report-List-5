<p><p>The medical imaging instrumentation market includes various types of imaging systems such as X-ray systems, ultrasound devices, MRI (magnetic resonance imaging), computed tomography (CT), nuclear imaging, and others. X-ray systems use electromagnetic radiation to create images of the inside of the body. Ultrasound devices use sound waves to generate images. MRI uses powerful magnets and radio waves to create detailed images. CT scanning uses X-rays and a computer to produce cross-sectional images. Nuclear imaging involves injecting a radioactive substance into the body to produce images. Other types of imaging systems may include positron emission tomography (PET) and mammography.</p></p>
