<p><p>Automotive harness assemblies are categorized into different types based on their specific functions within a vehicle. Body wiring harnesses connect various electrical components within the body of the vehicle. Chassis wiring harnesses connect components within the chassis, such as steering and suspension systems. Engine wiring harnesses are responsible for connecting electrical components within the engine. HVAC wiring harnesses control heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. Speed sensor wiring harnesses connect sensors that monitor vehicle speed. Other types of harness assemblies may include transmission wiring harnesses, fuel injection wiring harnesses, and navigation system wiring harnesses.</p></p>
