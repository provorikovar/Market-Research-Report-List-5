<p><p>The Swiss CNC lathe market can be categorized into two main types: Single-spindle lathe and Multi-spindle lathe. Single-spindle lathes have one spindle that holds and rotates the workpiece, allowing for precise cutting and shaping of the material. On the other hand, Multi-spindle lathes have multiple spindles that can work simultaneously on different parts of the material, increasing efficiency and productivity. Both types of lathes are widely used in the manufacturing industry for turning and shaping metal components with high precision.</p></p>
