<p><p>Automatic swing-door operators come in three main types: Full Energy Operators, Low Energy Operators, and Power Assist Operators. Full Energy Operators utilize 100% of their energy to open and close doors quickly, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Low Energy Operators use only 30% of their energy, making them more energy-efficient and suitable for low-traffic areas. Power Assist Operators provide a combination of manual and automatic operation, making them versatile for various settings and user preferences.</p></p>
