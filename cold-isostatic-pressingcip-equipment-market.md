<p><p>Cold Isostatic Pressing (CIP) Equipment market is broadly divided into two types, Dry Bag Pressing and Wet Bag Pressing. Dry Bag Pressing involves placing the powdered material into a bag before pressing, while Wet Bag Pressing involves using a wet slurry of the material. Dry Bag Pressing is preferred for materials that can be easily compacted, while Wet Bag Pressing is used for materials that are difficult to compact in dry form. Both types of CIP equipment cater to specific requirements of different industries.</p></p>
