<p><p>Satellite-based Automatic Identification Systems (AIS) are used for tracking vessels at sea. Class A transponders are mandatory for larger vessels and transmit more data, such as vessel identity, position, speed, and course. Class B transponders are optional for smaller vessels and transmit less data than Class A transponders. Both types of transponders use satellites to communicate their information, allowing for real-time tracking and monitoring of maritime traffic. The market for these systems is driven by regulatory requirements, safety concerns, and efficiency in maritime operations.</p></p>
