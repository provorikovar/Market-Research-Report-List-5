<p><ul><li>Hand-held Hook</li><li>Automatic Hook</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chest retractor market types include hand-held hook and automatic hook retractors. Hand-held hook retractors are manually operated instruments used in surgeries to hold back tissues and organs, offering precise control to the surgeon. On the other hand, automatic hook retractors are mechanized instruments that can be easily adjusted and locked into place, providing consistent tension throughout the procedure. Both types of retractors play a crucial role in ensuring clear visibility and access to the surgical site during chest surgeries.</p></p>
