<p><p>Concrete power buggies are available in two main types: tracked and wheeled. Tracked power buggies are equipped with rubber tracks, providing better traction and stability on rough or uneven terrain. They are ideal for jobsites with challenging conditions. Wheeled power buggies, on the other hand, feature pneumatic tires that are suitable for smooth surfaces and easy maneuverability. They are commonly used on construction sites with paved or flat ground. Both types offer efficient transport of concrete and other materials on job sites.</p></p>
