<p><ul><li>Mesophilic</li><li>Thermophilic</li><li>Probiotics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dairy starter culture market is divided into three main types: mesophilic, thermophilic, and probiotics. Mesophilic cultures thrive at lower temperatures, such as in the production of soft cheeses. Thermophilic cultures are used for higher temperature fermentation, like in the making of yogurt and hard cheeses. Probiotics are live bacteria that provide health benefits and are added to dairy products like yogurt. Each type of starter culture plays a crucial role in determining the flavor, texture, and nutritional value of dairy products.</p></p>
