<p><p>Oligonucleotide synthesis services market includes various types such as primers, probes, intermediate-scale synthesis oligos, large-scale synthesis oligos, linkers, and adaptors. Primers are short nucleic acid sequences used for DNA replication or PCR. Probes are used to detect specific sequences in a sample. Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos are mid-sized oligonucleotides used for research purposes. Large-scale synthesis oligos are longer sequences used in various applications. Linkers and adaptors are used to attach different molecules or sequences together in molecular biology experiments.</p></p>
