<p><p>The spindle shaper market can be divided into two main types: single spindle shapers and double spindle shapers. Single spindle shapers are more common and suitable for smaller woodworking tasks, while double spindle shapers are ideal for larger production volumes and more complex shaping requirements.</p></p>
