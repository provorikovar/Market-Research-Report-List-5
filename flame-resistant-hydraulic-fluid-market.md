<p><strong>The Flame Resistant Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame resistant hydraulic fluids are categorized into four types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids consist of water and fire-resistant additives, making them suitable for indoor use. HFB fluids are water-in-oil emulsions and are more stable than HFA fluids. HFC fluids are water-based and offer good lubrication properties. HFD fluids are synthetic esters and are the most fire-resistant type. Each type has specific properties and applications, catering to different industries and operating conditions.</p></p>
