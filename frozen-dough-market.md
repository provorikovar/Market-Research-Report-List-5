<p><p>Frozen dough market offers various types such as pre-fermented, pre-baked, unfermented, and fully-baked options. Pre-fermented frozen dough is partially fermented before freezing, allowing for a quicker baking process. Pre-baked frozen dough is partially baked before freezing, needing only finishing touches before consumption. Unfermented frozen dough is raw and requires proofing before baking. Fully-baked frozen dough is fully cooked before freezing, needing only to be reheated before serving. Each type offers convenience and flexibility for different baking needs.</p></p>
