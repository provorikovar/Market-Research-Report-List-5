<p><p>Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer(OADM) are devices used in optical communication networks to add or drop specific wavelengths of light. There are two main types of OADM markets - Fixed Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer, which have a fixed configuration and cannot be changed, and Reconfigurable Optical Add-Drop Multiplexer, which allow for dynamic adjustment of wavelengths. Fixed OADMs are typically used in simpler network setups, while Reconfigurable OADMs are more flexible and can adapt to changing network requirements.</p></p>
