<p><p>Oxygen Barrier PEX Pipe comes in three different types: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa pipes are cross-linked using the peroxide method, providing high flexibility and resistance to stress cracking. PE-Xb pipes are cross-linked using the silane method, offering excellent temperature and pressure resistance. PE-Xc pipes are cross-linked using the electron beam method, ensuring uniform cross-linking for enhanced durability. Each type caters to different needs and applications in the oxygen barrier PEX pipe market, providing options for various project requirements.</p></p>
