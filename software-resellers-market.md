<p><p>Software resellers operate in two main market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. In the Cloud-Based market, software is delivered over the internet, allowing access from anywhere with an internet connection. This model offers scalability, flexibility, and automatic updates. On the other hand, the On-Premises market involves installing software directly onto a company's servers and computers. This model provides full control over data and security but requires more maintenance and upfront costs. The choice between these two market types depends on a company's specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
