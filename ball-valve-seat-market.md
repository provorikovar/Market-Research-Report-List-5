<p><p>Ball valve seats are essential components that provide sealing and control flow in ball valves. There are two main types of ball valve seats in the market - soft valve seat and metal valve seat. Soft valve seats are made from elastomeric materials like PTFE or Teflon, offering excellent sealing properties but may wear out faster. Metal valve seats, on the other hand, are made from durable materials like stainless steel or brass, providing superior strength and longevity. The choice between these two types depends on specific application requirements and performance expectations.</p></p>
