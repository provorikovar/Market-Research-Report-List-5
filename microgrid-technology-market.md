<p><strong>The Microgrid Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grid-Tied Type Microgrid</li><li>Independent Type Microgrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microgrid Technology Market consists of two main types: Grid-Tied Type Microgrid and Independent Type Microgrid. Grid-Tied Type Microgrid is connected to the main utility grid and can both draw power from and supply power to the grid as needed. Independent Type Microgrid operates autonomously and is not connected to the main grid, relying on its own power sources such as renewable energy systems and energy storage. Both types offer benefits such as increased energy reliability, efficiency, and flexibility for users in various applications.</p></p>
