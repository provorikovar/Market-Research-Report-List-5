<p><p>Robotic Case Packing Systems come in two primary types: Side Load and Top Load. Side Load systems utilize robotic arms to pack products into cases from the side, typically accommodating a higher throughput. Top Load systems, on the other hand, pack products into cases from the top, offering greater flexibility in handling various product sizes and shapes. Both types are popular in the market, with Side Load systems being preferred for speed and efficiency, while Top Load systems excel in versatility and adaptability.</p></p>
