<p><p>Scientific lasers market can be segmented into four main types based on the medium used for generating the laser beam. Solid state lasers utilize solid materials such as crystals or glasses to produce the laser beam. Gas lasers use gases like carbon dioxide or helium-neon for the laser production. Liquid lasers employ liquid materials for generating the laser beam. Semiconductor lasers use semiconductors like gallium arsenide for producing laser light. Each type has unique properties and is used in various scientific applications.</p></p>
