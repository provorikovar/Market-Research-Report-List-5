<p><p>The fruit and vegetable powder market consists of three main types: fruit powder, vegetable powder, and fruit and vegetable mixed powder. Fruit powder is made by drying and grinding fruits into a fine powder, while vegetable powder is made using the same process with vegetables. Fruit and vegetable mixed powder combines both fruits and vegetables into a single powder product. These powders are popular for their convenience, long shelf life, and ability to retain the nutritional benefits of fresh fruits and vegetables.</p></p>
