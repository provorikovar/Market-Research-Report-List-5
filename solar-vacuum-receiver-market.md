The market can be classified into two main types: medium temperature vacuum receivers and high temperature vacuum receivers. Medium temperature receivers are designed to operate at lower temperature ranges, typically up to 400°C, while high temperature receivers are capable of operating at much higher temperatures, up to 1000°C. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency of solar power systems by capturing and transferring solar energy effectively.</p></p>
