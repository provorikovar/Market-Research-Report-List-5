<p><p>Radiographic testing is a non-destructive testing method used to detect internal defects in materials. The market includes various types such as Film Radiography, Real Time Radiography (RTR), Computed Tomography (CT), Digital Radiography (DR), and Computed Radiography (CR). Film radiography involves capturing images on film, while real-time radiography provides instant results. Computed tomography creates 3D images, and digital radiography uses digital sensors for image capture. Computed radiography is similar to digital radiography but uses phosphor plates instead of digital sensors.</p></p>
