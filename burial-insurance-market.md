<p><ul><li>Level Death Benefit</li><li>Modified or Graded Death Benefit</li><li>Guaranteed Acceptance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Burial insurance, also known as final expense insurance, offers different market types to meet diverse needs. Level death benefit policies provide a fixed payout amount upon the insured's passing. Modified or graded death benefit policies have a waiting period before full coverage kicks in, usually within the first few years of the policy. Guaranteed acceptance policies do not require a medical exam and are available to anyone, regardless of health status. These market types cater to individuals seeking financial protection for funeral expenses and final arrangements.</p></p>
