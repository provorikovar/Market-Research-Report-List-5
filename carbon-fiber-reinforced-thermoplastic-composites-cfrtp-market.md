<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic Composites (CFRTP) are a type of advanced material that combines carbon fibers with a thermoplastic polymer matrix. There are two main types of CFRTP on the market: Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based and Pitch-based. PAN-based CFRTPs are derived from a precursor material called polyacrylonitrile, which is processed into carbon fibers. Pitch-based CFRTPs, on the other hand, are made from coal tar or petroleum-derived pitches. Both types offer high strength, stiffness, and lightweight properties, making them ideal for use in various industries.</p></p>
