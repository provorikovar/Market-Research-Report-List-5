<p><strong>The Carrot Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Drying Carrot Powder</li><li>Freeze-dried Carrot Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spray drying carrot powder is produced by spraying liquid carrot concentrate onto hot air, resulting in a fine powder. It is cost-effective and has a longer shelf life compared to other drying methods. On the other hand, freeze-dried carrot powder is produced by freezing the carrot puree and then drying it in a vacuum, preserving the color, flavor, and nutrients of the carrot. It is more expensive due to the complex process but retains more of the original qualities of the carrot.</p></p>
