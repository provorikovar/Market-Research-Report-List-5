<p><ul><li>Surface Acoustic Wave(SAW) Sensor</li><li>Bulk Acoustic Wave(BAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acoustic wave sensors are broadly classified into two types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensor and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) sensor. SAW sensors use surface acoustic waves to detect and measure changes in the environment, such as pressure or temperature. On the other hand, BAW sensors utilize bulk acoustic waves that travel through a solid material to detect various parameters. Both types of sensors have unique applications and advantages, leading to a diverse market for acoustic wave sensor technologies.</p></p>
