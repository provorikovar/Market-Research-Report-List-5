<p><p>In the truck assembly market, there are two main types of assembly processes: Full-Automatic and Semi-Automatic. Full-Automatic assembly involves minimal human intervention, with most of the assembly process being carried out by robots and machines. This type of assembly is known for its high efficiency and precision. On the other hand, Semi-Automatic assembly combines both machine and human labor, with workers being directly involved in certain steps of the assembly process. This type of assembly is often considered a middle-ground between fully automated and manual assembly processes.</p></p>
