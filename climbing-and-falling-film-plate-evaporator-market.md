<p><p>Climbing and falling film plate evaporators are used in both Single-Effect and Multiple-Effect configurations in the market. In Single-Effect evaporators, the entire process is carried out in a single stage. This is suitable for small-scale operations. On the other hand, Multiple-Effect evaporators have multiple stages where the vapor from one effect is used to heat the next effect, leading to higher efficiency and energy savings. Multiple-Effect evaporators are commonly used in large-scale industrial applications.</p></p>
