Return-to-invoice GAP Insurance pays the difference between the insurance settlement and the original purchase price. Vehicle Replacement GAP Insurance provides funds to replace a vehicle with a new one. Return-to-value GAP Insurance covers the depreciation of a vehicle's value. Other types of GAP insurance may include coverage for leased vehicles or commercial vehicles.</p></p>
