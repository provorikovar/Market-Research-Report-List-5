<p><p>The environmental control room market includes two main types: temperature-controlled and humidity-controlled facilities. Temperature-controlled rooms maintain a stable temperature range to preserve products that are sensitive to temperature fluctuations, such as food, pharmaceuticals, and electronics. Meanwhile, humidity-controlled rooms regulate the level of moisture in the air to protect items that are prone to damage from excess humidity, like artwork and medical supplies. Both types of environmental control rooms are essential for maintaining optimal conditions for various industries and applications.</p></p>
