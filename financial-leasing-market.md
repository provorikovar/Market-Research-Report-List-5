<p><p>Financial leasing is a method of obtaining finance for the acquisition of assets without using up available credit lines. It involves a contract between a leasing company (lessor) and a lessee, wherein the lessee pays a series of installments over an agreed period of time in exchange for the use of the asset. At the end of the leasing period, the lessee has the option to purchase the asset at a predetermined price.</p><p>The financial leasing market is experiencing significant growth, driven by factors such as increasing demand for leasing services across various industries, favorable government policies supporting leasing activities, and the need for businesses to conserve cash flow and optimize their capital structure.
