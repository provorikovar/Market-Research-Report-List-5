<p><p>Physical stimuli responsive polymers are materials that can change their properties in response to specific external stimuli such as temperature, light, pressure, electric or magnetic fields. Thermo-responsive polymers can undergo reversible changes in their structure or properties in response to temperature changes. Photo-responsive polymers change their properties in response to light exposure. Pressure-responsive polymers alter their properties under mechanical pressure. Electroactive polymers respond to electric fields, while magnetically-responsive polymers change in the presence of a magnetic field. These materials have applications in various industries including healthcare, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
