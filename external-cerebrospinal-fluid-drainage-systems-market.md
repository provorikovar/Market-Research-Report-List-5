<p><ul><li>External Ventricular Drainage (EVD) Systems</li><li>Lumbar Drainage (LD) Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>External cerebrospinal fluid drainage systems are medical devices used to drain excess fluid from the brain or spinal cord. The two main types are External Ventricular Drainage (EVD) Systems and Lumbar Drainage (LD) Systems. EVD systems involve placing a catheter into the brain's ventricles to drain fluid, while LD systems involve inserting a catheter into the lumbar spine to drain fluid. These systems are crucial in managing conditions such as hydrocephalus, traumatic brain injury, and intracranial hemorrhage.</p></p>
