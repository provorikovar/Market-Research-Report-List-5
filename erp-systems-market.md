<p><ul><li>On premise ERP</li><li>Cloud-based ERP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>On-premise ERP systems are installed and managed on a company's own servers and infrastructure, requiring a dedicated IT team for maintenance. Cloud-based ERP systems, on the other hand, are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through a web browser, offering flexibility and scalability without the need for extensive IT resources. The on-premise ERP market caters to organizations with strict data security requirements and the resources to manage an in-house system, while the cloud-based ERP market appeals to businesses looking for cost-effective and easily scalable solutions.</p></p>
