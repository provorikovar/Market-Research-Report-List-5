<p><p>Regenerative turbine pumps are classified into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage. Single-stage regenerative turbine pumps are designed with only one impeller and are suitable for applications requiring low to moderate pressure. On the other hand, multi-stage regenerative turbine pumps have multiple impellers and are ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types of pumps are commonly used in industries such as water treatment, chemical processing, and HVAC systems to efficiently move fluids and generate pressure.</p></p>
