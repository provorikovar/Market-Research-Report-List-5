There are three main types of catheter securement devices: portal, epidural, and peripheral. Portal catheter securement devices are used to secure central venous catheters, epidural catheter securement devices are used for spinal epidural catheters, and peripheral catheter securement devices are used for catheters inserted into peripheral veins. Each type of device is designed to ensure the catheter remains in place and reduces the risk of displacement or infection.</p></p>
