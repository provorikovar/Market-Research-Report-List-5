<p><p>Karl Fischer reagents are commonly used for moisture determination in various industries. There are two main types of Karl Fischer reagents: volumetric reagent and coulometric reagent. Volumetric reagents are used for traditional titration methods, where the amount of water is determined by the volume of reagent added. Coulometric reagents, on the other hand, utilize an electrochemical cell to generate iodine for the titration process. Both types of reagents have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific requirements of the moisture determination process.</p></p>
