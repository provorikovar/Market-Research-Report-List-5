<p><p>Abrasive waterjet cutting machines can be categorized into two main types: Cantilever and Gantry. Cantilever machines have one side supported by a single beam, allowing for easier access to the workpiece. Gantry machines, on the other hand, are supported by two beams spanning the entire work area, providing greater stability and precision. The choice between the two types depends on factors such as the size of the workpiece, level of precision required, and budget considerations.</p></p>
