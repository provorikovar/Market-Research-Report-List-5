<p><p>Nuclear pumps market consists of various types of pumps used in nuclear power plants. Reactor coolant pumps are used to circulate coolant within the reactor, ensuring that the core remains at a safe temperature. Condensate pumps are responsible for transferring condensed steam back into the boiler for reuse. Feed water pumps deliver water to the boiler for steam generation. Other types of pumps in the market include emergency cooling pumps and auxiliary pumps, all crucial for the safe and efficient operation of nuclear power plants.</p></p>
