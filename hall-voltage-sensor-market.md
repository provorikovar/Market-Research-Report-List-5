<p><p>Hall Voltage Sensors are used for measuring magnetic fields in various applications. Analog Output Hall Sensors provide a continuous output signal proportional to the magnetic field strength, while Digital Output Hall Sensors provide a binary output signal indicating the presence or absence of a magnetic field. Analog sensors are preferred for precise measurements, while digital sensors are commonly used for simple proximity detection. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in various industries such as automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
