<p><p>Axle counters are used for train detection in railway systems and come in two main market types: Rail Side Axle Counter and On-rail Axle Counter. Rail Side Axle Counters are installed beside the tracks and use sensors to detect trains passing by. On-rail Axle Counters are placed directly on the railway tracks and use sensors to count the number of axles as trains pass over them. Both types of axle counters are essential for accurate train detection and ensuring safe and efficient railway operations.</p></p>
