<p><p>Air Conditioning Electronic Expansion Valves (EEV) are key components in air conditioning systems, controlling the flow of refrigerant to ensure optimal cooling. Two main types are Electromagnetic EEVs, which use a solenoid coil to regulate refrigerant flow, and Electric EEVs, which use a stepper motor for precise control. The Electromagnetic EEV market is characterized by its simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while the Electric EEV market offers greater precision and efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and energy efficiency of air conditioning systems.</p></p>
