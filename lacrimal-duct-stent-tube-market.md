<p><p>Lacrimal duct stent tubes are used to treat conditions that cause blockages in the tear drainage system of the eye. There are two main types of lacrimal duct stent tubes: Monocanalicular and Bicanalicular. Monocanalicular stents are placed in one of the tear ducts, while bicanalicular stents are placed in both tear ducts for more complex cases. Both types of stent tubes help to create a pathway for tears to drain properly, reducing symptoms and complications associated with tear duct blockages.</p></p>
