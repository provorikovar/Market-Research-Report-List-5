<p><p>The indoor humidity sensor market is divided into two main types: wired indoor humidity sensors and wireless indoor humidity sensors. Wired sensors are connected directly to a central system through a physical cable, providing real-time data on humidity levels. Wireless sensors, on the other hand, use radio frequency technology to transmit data wirelessly to a central hub, offering more flexibility in placement and easier installation. Both types of sensors are crucial for maintaining optimal humidity levels indoors for health, comfort, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
