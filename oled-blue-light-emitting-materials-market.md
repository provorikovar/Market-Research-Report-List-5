<p><p>OLED Blue Light Emitting Materials Market includes four main types: Fluorescent Emitter, TADF Emitter, Hyperfluorescence Emitter, and Phosphorescent Emitter (Light Blue for Lighting). Fluorescent Emitters are the traditional method of producing blue light but have lower efficiency. TADF Emitters are a newer technology that improves efficiency by harvesting both singlet and triplet excitons. Hyperfluorescence Emitters are materials with high photoluminescence quantum yields. Phosphorescent Emitters, specifically for light blue lighting, have longer excited state lifetimes leading to higher efficiency.</p></p>
