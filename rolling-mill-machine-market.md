<p><p>Rolling Mill Machines are used in metalworking to shape and compress metal into various forms. Two main types are Hot Rolling Mills and Cold Rolling Mills. Hot Rolling Mills heat the metal above its recrystallization temperature, making it easier to shape, while Cold Rolling Mills process the metal at room temperature, resulting in a smoother finish. Both types play crucial roles in industries such as automotive, construction, and aerospace by producing sheet metal, bars, and other metal products.</p></p>
