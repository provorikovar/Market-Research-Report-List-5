<p><strong>The Dry Dairy Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Dry Dairy Powder</li><li>Low Fat Dry Dairy Powder</li><li>Skimmed Dry Dairy Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry Dairy Powder Market can be categorized into three types:</p><p>1. Whole Dry Dairy Powder: This type contains the highest amount of fat and is made by drying whole milk. It is rich and creamy in flavor, making it ideal for products like ice cream and yogurt.</p><p>2. Low Fat Dry Dairy Powder: This type has a lower fat content compared to whole dry dairy powder. It is commonly used in baking and cooking applications where a lighter taste is desired.</p><p>3. Skimmed Dry Dairy Powder: This type contains the least amount of fat and is made by removing almost all of the fat from milk. It is often used in producing low-fat dairy products and as a milk substitute in various food products.</p></p>
