<p><p>Telephone answering machines come in two main types: corded answering machines and cordless answering machines.</p><p>Corded answering machines are typically stationary devices that are connected directly to a landline telephone. They are ideal for home or office use where a phone is always located in the same spot.</p><p>Cordless answering machines, on the other hand, offer more flexibility as they can be placed anywhere within the range of a base station. They are popular for households or businesses with multiple rooms or floors.</p></p>
