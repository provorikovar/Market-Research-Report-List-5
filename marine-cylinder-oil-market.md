<p><p>Marine Cylinder Oil market can be classified into two main types based on their Base Number (BN): High-BN (70-100 BN) and Low-BN (15-60 BN) oils. High-BN oils are recommended for use in high-sulfur fuel conditions to neutralize acidic combustion by-products and prevent engine corrosion. On the other hand, Low-BN oils are suitable for newer engines designed to operate with lower sulfur fuels. Choosing the right type of oil is crucial for maintaining engine performance and preventing damage.</p></p>
