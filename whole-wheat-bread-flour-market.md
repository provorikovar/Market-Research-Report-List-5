<p><p>Whole wheat bread flour can be divided into two main types: machine whole wheat bread flour and stone whole wheat bread flour. Machine whole wheat bread flour is ground with modern machinery, resulting in a more consistent texture and finer grain. On the other hand, stone whole wheat bread flour is ground using traditional stone mills, which can retain more nutrients and offer a coarser texture. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with machine whole wheat bread flour being more commonly found in the market.</p></p>
